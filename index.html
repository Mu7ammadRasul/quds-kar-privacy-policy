Privacy Policy

Quds Kar — Committed to protecting your privacy and dignity

Last updated: October 22, 2025

At Quds Kar, a charity based in Iraqi Kurdistan, we prioritize the privacy and security of everyone who interacts with our organization. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we keep it safe.

Information we collect

We only collect information you provide voluntarily to help us offer support and coordinate assistance.

  • Basic identity
    Name, phone number
  • Location
    Latitude & longitude when you use location features
  • Support details
    Category and type of assistance requested
  • Donations (limited)
    Donated amount when provided. We do not store full payment details in-app.

We do not collect device fingerprints or analytics by default. If such data are needed in the future to improve services, we will update this policy and notify users where required.

How we use information

  • To provide and coordinate requested assistance.
  • To contact you about your request or donation.
  • To improve our services and prevent misuse or fraud.
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations.
Note: We will not sell your personal information. Any sharing with trusted partners (for example medical providers) will be limited to what's necessary to provide assistance and only when you consent or when legally required.

How we protect your information

We implement appropriate technical and organizational safeguards such as SSL/TLS for data in transit, role-based access controls, and secure hosting practices. Access to personal data is restricted to authorized staff who need it to perform their duties.

Security practices (summary)
  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/SSL)
  • Least-privilege access controls
  • Regular backups and secure storage
  • Incident response procedures

Your rights

You may access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. You can also opt out of non-essential communications. To exercise these rights, contact us at info.dev.tech.help@gmail.com. We aim to respond to requests promptly and in accordance with applicable law.

Changes to this Privacy Policy
