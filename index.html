Quds Kar (Charity) — protecting your privacy and personal information

At Quds Kar, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and use personal information and the steps we take to protect your privacy. Last updated: October 22, 2025.

Quds Kar (Charity) is a charitable organization based in Iraqi Kurdistan that focuses on helping people in need, especially those requiring medical treatment and those affected by poverty. We take your privacy seriously and describe below what information we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

At Quds Kar, we only collect the personal information that you provide to us voluntarily, for example when you request help, register, or communicate with us:

  • Your name
  • Phone number
  • Location information (latitude and longitude) when you use our location services
  • Information about the category of help you need
  • Information about the type of help you need

We only collect the personal information provided by users, including their name, phone number, and donated amount when applicable. We do not collect additional device information (such as device type or operating system) or usage analytics by default. We do not collect financial or full payment details within our app.

We use the personal information we collect to provide the services you request, to communicate with you about your account or our services, and — with your consent — to send you informational emails and newsletters. We may also use your information to improve our services, to prevent fraud or illegal activities, and to comply with legal requirements.

How we protect your information

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. For example, we use industry-standard encryption (such as SSL/TLS) to protect data transmitted to our services and limit access to authorized personnel only.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can also opt out of receiving promotional emails and newsletters at any time. To exercise these rights, please contact us at info.dev.tech.help@gmail.com.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we update the policy, we will post the revised version here and update the "Last updated" date at the top.

Contact us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our practices regarding your personal information, please contact us at info.dev.tech.help@gmail.com.
